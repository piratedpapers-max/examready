Photo size requirements for all major Indian exams

Every competitive exam in India — whether it is UPSC Civil Services, BPSC, BSSC, Bihar Police, SSC CGL, Railway NTPC, NEET, or JEE Main — has its own specific requirements for passport-size photograph and signature. Getting these wrong is one of the most common reasons for form rejection.

ExamReady is a free tool built specifically for students in Bihar and across India who are preparing for government job exams (sarkari naukri), medical and engineering entrance exams, and banking exams. You can resize, compress, and convert your photo and signature to exact exam specifications in seconds — without going to a cyber café.

Our database covers photo size for BPSC (213×284 px), UPSC (200×230 px), BSSC (150×200 px), SSC (100×120 px), NEET (200×230 px), JEE Main (200×230 px), Railway NTPC (130×150 px), IBPS PO (200×230 px), SBI PO (200×200 px), Bihar Police (213×284 px), and many more.
