About ExamReady

ExamReady is a free toolkit built for students preparing for UPSC, BPSC, BSSC, Bihar Police, SSC CGL, Railway NTPC, NEET, JEE, IBPS, SBI and other competitive exams in India — especially Bihar and UP.

All image processing happens inside your browser. Your photos are never uploaded to any server. The tool is completely free with no registration required.
